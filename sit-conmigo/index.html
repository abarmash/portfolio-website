Sit Conmigo (Concept Client) | Responsive Website Design

Tools Used: Adobe XD

Project Overview: Sit Conmigo is a chair manufacturer that focuses on ethically-built and sustainably-sourced chairs. Sit Conmigo has four chair designs and were ready to launch a website so that customers can pre-order them. They were looking for a single-page website with sections for Pre-Order, About, and Contact, as well as a large header with a call-to-action button encouraging customers to pre-order.

Project Deliverables: Mobile Design Comp, Tablet Design Comp, Desktop Design Comp

Project Goals: There were three project goals for Sit Conmigo’s new website:
1. Showcase the four chair designs and entice customers to pre-order them.
2. Highlight Sit Conmigo’s mission as a fair trade and fair labor chair maker.
3. Visually communicate Sit Conmigo’s personality and mission.

Design Process: After learning about the client’s needs, I started by developing the site structure and drawing a paper wireframe for mobile, tablet, and desktop. Then, I built a digital wireframe for mobile, tablet, and desktop, chose the typography, developed the color scheme using provided images of the available chairs, and built a hi-fi design composition. After receiving feedback on the design comps from users, I made a few revisions and handed off the final design files.
