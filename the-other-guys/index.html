The Other Guys Coffee (Concept Client) | Branding, Identity Design, and Landing Page Design

Tools Used: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Figma

Project Overview: The Other Guys is a new coffee shop opening in the Washington, DC area by a local business owner. I was hired to develop a brand and identity for the new coffee shop, as well as to design a landing page for the coffee shop’s grand opening.

Project Deliverables: Brand Name, Brand Strategy and Research, Logo Design, Pair of Icons, Social Media Headers, Brand Style Guide, Landing Page Design

Project Goals: There were three project goals for The Other Guys:
1. To design and develop the brand identity including the brand’s name, personality, mission statement, target audience, logo, and style guide.
2. Attract as many customers to the coffee shop as possible during its grand opening weekend (to be met by advertising the grand opening on the landing page).
3. Form long-term relationships with locals and customers so the coffee shop is top of mind (to be met by a call-to-action on the landing page for users to sign up for the shop’s monthly newsletter in exchange for a free scone).

Design Process: After learning about the client’s needs and setting the project goals, I started by selecting the coffee shop’s name, developed the brand’s personality, wrote the brand’s mission statement, and researched the target audience. Then, I developed the brand’s typescale and color scheme, designed The Other Guys’ logo, and developed the brand’s style guide. For the landing page, I first drew a paper wireframe, then built a digital wireframe, and used the brand’s style guide to build a hi-fi design composition. After receiving feedback on the landing page design from users, I made a few revisions and handed off the final design files.
