About Me

I’ve been designing and creating non-stop since I was a kid, moving from markers and paper to photography using disposable Kodak cameras, redecorating my bedroom each season, and teaching myself calligraphy. Now, I specialize in web design, branding and identity design, and graphic design. I am passionate about creating design solutions that look great, enhance users' experience, and are a joy to use. I enjoy working in a variety of mediums and especially love to incorporate hand-crafted logotypes, scripts, and other art elements.

I am also experienced in UX Research and think it should be a major component of all design projects. I try to take on a user-focused approach for every project and have experience in all aspects of the UX Research and Design cycle, including user interviews, journey maps, user flows, split A/B testing, card sorting, wireframing, designing paper and digital prototypes, and conducting usability tests.

When I’m not designing, you can find me in the woods with a camera in hand, playing with my cats, or rewatching New Girl.
